About the Nintendo 3DS

The Nintendo 3DS is the 5th Nintendo DS system. It comes after the Nintendo DS, Nintendo DS lite, Nintendo DSi, and the Nintendo DSi XL. There have been a lot of changes since the first Nintendo DS (for example: Camera in the DSi). But the Nintendo 3DS has a lot of new stuff! Continue reading, and discover the Nintendo 3DS!

So what is the Nintendo 3DS? Well, the 3DS is the first 3D sight gaming console in the world! And there are no 3D glasses needed! A new dimension in gaming has been discovered!


The Nintendo will be released 27 March 2011 in the USA and 25 March 2011 in Europe. The price will be $249.99 (€249.99).

Here is a picture of the Nintendo 3DS, with some highlighted parts:


This is what the parts are:

1: Two outer cameras: There are two outer cameras, to take 3D pictures, and for mixing the real world with games in 3D.

2: Inner camera: There is an inner camera, so you can take pictures of yourself or you can put yourself in a game.

3: 3D screen: The upper screen is in 3D, so you can play your games or watch movies, ect. in 3D, without the need of 3D glasses!

4: 3D adjust button: This is a button to adjust the 3D from a lot of 3D to no 3D (2D) precisely

5: Analog circle pad: To make precise movements in games.

6: Home button: Press this button while playing a game to go to the home screen, you can do something else now, and continue where you stopped playing!

7: Spot Pass/ Street Pass Notificator: If this lamp is on, you have a new Spot Pass or Street Pass notification
